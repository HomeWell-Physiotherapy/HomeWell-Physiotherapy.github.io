The In-Home Difference

We know that those in need of physiotherapy often have mobility issues that prevent them from traveling to a traditional clinic, or even leaving their home. We bring physiotherapy services to those clients and develop treatment plans for within the comfort of their own homes.

Please note, HomeWell Physiotherapy services are unfortunately not covered under MCP. You will be responsible for payment for your appointments. If you have private health insurance with physiotherapy coverage, in-home sessions are included and we offer direct billing for most insurance companies.

Please contact us for more details on pricing and payment process.
